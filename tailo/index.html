Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Tailo ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Tailo is designed to minimize unnecessary data collection. Some app data is stored locally on your device, and some account and pet care data may be stored on our server.

  • Account Information: Email, name, language, account data
  • Pet Care Data: Pet profiles, health records, routines
  • Pet Sharing Data: Shared access data
  • Local Media: Stored only on device
  • Advertising Data: Google AdMob
  • Purchase Information: RevenueCat

How We Use Information

  • Show ads
